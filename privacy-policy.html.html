Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2025
ChennaiOpp is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Device information — device type, operating system version
  • Usage data — which opportunities you view and search for
  • Notification preferences — which categories you want alerts for
  • Location (optional) — only if you grant permission, to show nearby opportunities

We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identification unless you voluntarily provide it.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Show you relevant educational opportunities in Chennai
  • Send push notifications about new opportunities you've subscribed to
  • Improve the app experience and fix bugs
  • Understand which categories are most useful to students

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Google AdMob — for showing relevant ads (has its own privacy policy)
  • Firebase — for push notifications (Google's service)
  • Legal requirements — if required by law

4. Advertisements

ChennaiOpp uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

5. Data Storage

Your notification preferences are stored locally on your device. Usage analytics are stored securely on our servers hosted on Render (USA). We retain this data for a maximum of 12 months.

6. Children's Privacy

ChennaiOpp is designed for students aged 15 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of push notifications anytime in app settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Our backend uses HTTPS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Third Party Links

Our app contains links to external websites (colleges, government portals, companies). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
